    Product & Audience Strategy

    Product Overview: This tool is a real-time, AI-assisted classroom discussion platform designed for university-level Pharmacology lectures. It "listens" to live lectures and "sees" presentation content to generate AI-driven discussion prompts. Instructors use it to foster interactive learning, while students use it to submit anonymous, friction-free responses.

    Target Audience Personas

    Our UI must accommodate two distinct groups with opposite needs and environments.

    1. The Instructor (The Broadcaster)

    • Environment: Standing in a live lecture hall, high cognitive load, managing time, technology, and student engagement simultaneously.
    • Device: Desktop/Laptop (often projected onto a large screen).
    • Primary UI Needs:
      • High Control & Visibility: They need to see exactly what the AI is generating and what the students are seeing at a glance.
      • Forgiveness: Live environments are stressful. They need clear undo buttons and easy ways to edit AI prompts before sending them (Error Prevention).
      • Efficiency: Lesson management and post-session analytics must be straightforward and easy to export.

    2. The Student (The Participant)

    • Environment: Sitting in a lecture hall, potentially distracted, using varying qualities of Wi-Fi/cellular data.
    • Device: Mobile-first (mostly smartphones), secondary laptops.
    • Primary UI Needs:
      • Zero Friction: No accounts, no passwords, no app downloads. Entry via QR code or simple PIN must take less than 5 seconds.
      • Trust & Anonymity: The UI must visually reassure them that their responses are 100% anonymous to encourage honest participation.
      • Lightweight: The interface must be minimal, loading instantly and focusing entirely on the current question.
